About us

"In the year 1917 on June 7.27 Clubs namely Indiana lions Clubs. The Reciprocity Club, Professional man, Business Center etc. met to from an association dedicated to serve the community under the leadership of Melvin Jones an insurance man from Chicago. By Secret ballot representatives of the Clubs Selected the Association of Lions Clubs in the Hotel La Salle, Chicago. The aim of the association to do Something for the Community not for self. At present 13.4 million Lions in 46000 Clubs in 208 Countries Serving 8 million people annually under 751 Lions District."
